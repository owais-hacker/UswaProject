About Physics


Physics is a fascinating subject that explores the fundamental principles governing our universe. In Class 9th and 10th, students are introduced to the basic concepts of physics, including motion, forces, energy, and more.

As students progress to Class 11th and 12th, physics becomes more specialized, covering topics such as electromagnetism, thermodynamics, quantum mechanics, and relativity. These classes provide a deeper understanding of the fundamental laws of nature.
